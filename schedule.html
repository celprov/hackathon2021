The schedule for this year was prepared to balance accessible times across the world and occasions for inter-continental collaborations, this is why we structured the event in two time-slots. We had a bit of fun with the names: as the day literally starts in eastern Asia and Oceania, we called Rising sun the time slot covering from 1AM UTC to 6AM UTC; for the western world, we settled for daytime in the Atlantic Ocean, and we called Atlantis the time slot covering from 2PM UTC to 7PM UTC.
