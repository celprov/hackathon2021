All the events organized by the Open-Science Special Interest Group of OHBM will be virtual, so we won't meet in person in Montreal this year. But we want the venue page of this brainhack to reflect the beauty and the diversity of places and people that can be found on our planet.

We would very much like for every brainhack attendee to add to this page a picture related to:

  • their home town or home country,
  • or the town or country they now live in,
  • or a place on this planet dear to their heart.

To do that you can follow the instruction in this issue to guide you on how to make a pull request to this repository to add your image.
